Temperature Facts

Temperature is a measure of how hot or cold something is, and it plays a vital role in our daily lives. The most common temperature scales are Celsius, Fahrenheit, and Kelvin. Water freezes at 0°C and boils at 100°C, while in Fahrenheit, those same points are 32°F and 212°F.

About Conversion

Temperature conversion is the process of changing a temperature reading from one scale to another, such as from Celsius to Fahrenheit or Kelvin. This is especially useful daily weather updates. For example, to convert Celsius to Fahrenheit, you multiply the temperature by 9/5 and add 32.
